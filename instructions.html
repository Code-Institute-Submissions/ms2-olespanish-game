¡Olé Spanish!

Instructions

¡Olé Spanish! memory game is a great way to practice Spanish vocabulary. This memory game will test both your vocabulary and your memory.

The purpose of the game is to find all the matching pairs of cards (before you run out of time). To do that, you first have to click on a card to burn it over, and then a second card to check if they match. If they do not match, the cards are turned back over. The game is over when the player has matched all of the pairs and all the cards are gone.

If you'd like to refresh the lesson's vocabulary before starting to play, just click on the Refresh vocabulary button down below or start playing right away!
